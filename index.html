Mamadou thiam

Dr in Atmospheric, Ocean and Climate Physics

I am a postdoctoral researcher in climate, atmospheric, and ocean sciences.

Postdoctoral Researcher at the Laboratory of Atmospheric and Ocean Sciences - Materials, Energy, and Devices (LSAO-MED), Gaston Berger University, Senegal, and Associate Researcher at the Laboratory for Atmospheres and Space Observations (LATMOS-IPSL), Sorbonne University, France. My research focuses on coastal ocean dynamics in West Africa, the impact of ocean forcing on the Sahelian climate, and forced modeling to analyze how the Atlantic Ocean influences atmospheric circulation in the Sahel.
